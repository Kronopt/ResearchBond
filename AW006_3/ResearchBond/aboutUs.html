About This Amazing App

ResearchBond is a Web Application designed for scientific or academic researchers to explore the impact of their work, individually and within a team. Considering the number of citations of a set of publications, the researcher is allowed to navigate through a network of interactions with its fellow co-authors.

With this form of visualization, researchers may assess the most profitable collaborations of fields of work, among their academic path.

Author's Impact

In the network, the size of the node representing the researcher is proportional to his/her h-index.

Connections' Strength

The author is connected to the nodes representing his/her co-authors by edges of variable length. The width of the edges represents the percentage of publications shared with the co-author.
